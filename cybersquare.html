Cybersquare learning platform

Every registered student will have a seperate login account to hte cybersquare learning platform.Here,they will be able to view their course progress,assignments provided by teachers,their batch performance,progress card and so on.They will be able to attend online exams and live classes. Students can attent live classes to clear their doubts and interact with teachers.Apart from this they will also get notifications on upcoming live classes,news and events that they dont miss them.

Teachers can also login into Cybersquare platform and able to view their performance,batch progress,make reports,evaluate their works such as assignments,projects,questions,etc.Teachers can schedule online classes and online exams also.
